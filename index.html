About This Book

📖 Comprehensive Coverage

Sixteen chapters covering the full spectrum of AI integration in AEC education, from foundational concepts to advanced applications and future trends.

🎯 Practical Resources

Each chapter includes supplementary material such as figures, code, appendices, and datasets designed for deeper learning.

🔬 Research-Based

Grounded in the latest research and best practices, combining theoretical frameworks with evidence-based pedagogical approaches.

🌍 Global Perspective

Drawing on international case studies and diverse educational contexts to provide a truly global view of AI in AEC education.
