Dr. M. Reza Hosseini

Faculty of Architecture, Building and Planning, The University of Melbourne

Dr. M. Reza Hosseini is a distinguished scholar and global leader in the intersection of artificial intelligence and construction technology. Currently serving as Senior Lecturer in Construction Technology at the University of Melbourne's Faculty of Architecture, Building and Planning, Dr. Hosseini has achieved remarkable recognition, ranking among the top 2% of researchers globally.

His scholarly output encompasses approximately 240 papers and book chapters, establishing him as a pioneering force in digitalized construction and AI education. As a Research Fellow at the Centre for Research in Assessment and Digital Learning (CRADLE), Dr. Hosseini bridges the critical gap between AI technology and educational innovation.

He is the founder and leader of the Australian BIM Academic Forum (ABAF), establishing him as a key figure in shaping BIM and AI education across Australia. His seminal work, "BIM Teaching and Learning Handbook," provides the essential framework for integrating Building Information Modelling with AI technologies.

About This Book

📖 Comprehensive Coverage

Ten structured chapters covering the full spectrum of AI integration in AEC education, from foundational concepts to advanced applications and future trends.

🎯 Practical Resources

Each chapter includes exercise files, presentation slides, assessment rubrics, and hands-on activities designed for immediate classroom implementation.

🔬 Research-Based

Grounded in the latest research and best practices, combining theoretical frameworks with evidence-based pedagogical approaches.

🌍 Global Perspective

Drawing on international case studies and diverse educational contexts to provide a truly global view of AI in AEC education.
