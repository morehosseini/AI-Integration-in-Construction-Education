# Chapter 1: Foundations of AI in AEC - Complete Implementation Guide

## Chapter Overview

**Title:** Foundations of AI in AEC  
**Duration:** 4-6 weeks (12-18 contact hours)  
**Level:** Introductory  
**Prerequisites:** Basic understanding of construction processes

## Learning Objectives

By the end of this chapter, students will be able to:
1. Define artificial intelligence and explain its core concepts
2. Distinguish between different types of machine learning approaches
3. Identify current and potential applications of AI in AEC
4. Evaluate the transformative potential of AI in the built environment
5. Apply basic AI concepts to construction-related problems
